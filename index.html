Work
This is a selection of my professional and personal projects.

Eric J. Phillips Law

with The Law Office of Eric J. Phillips

I worked with Eric and Nancy Phillips to design and develop this website and logo to showcase the independent practice of The Law Office of Eric J. Phillips.

Castle

my Personal Project / School of Visual Arts

This interactive scene was created as a prototype for the first scene of a point-and-click fairy tale themed game. Interactions include the click and drag clouds, mouseover trees, and mouseover windmills.

Wild Swans

my MFA Thesis Project / School of Visual Arts

Wild Swans was created as my MFA thesis project in the Computer Art department at the School of Visual Arts. I illustrated and coded this augmented reality project using ActionScript 3, PaperVision3D, and FLARToolKit. Markers were printed on paper and held up to the camera, telling the tale of The Wild Swans.

Android in Space

with Google / DoubleClick Mobile Rich Media

Android in Space is a HTML5 mobile web display ad. It features the Samsung Nexus S, and is intended to be viewed on an iPad. I developed this unit in collaboration other members of the DoubleClick Rich Media team at Google.

Arrive Air Mixer

with Transistor Studios / Sears

This interactive expanding unit was created as part of the Sears Arrive Lounge Air Band campaign. The user was able to drag and drop various video and sound clips of each air dancer, and play back the sequence at the end. I worked as the lead developer on this unit at Transistor Studios. I worked closely with the visual designer, video editor, and art director on the ad development. The unit was trafficked through PointRoll.

Essential Fives

with Transistor Studios / Häagen-Dazs

These Häagen-Dazs units were developed as part of the larger E5 campaign, encouraging users to write down their top five items in a five-year time capsule to promote the new Häagen-Dazs Five ice cream. I worked as the lead animator and developer on these ads at Transistor Studios. The animations are entirely code-based, and the units were trafficked through PointRoll.

Quaker Experience

with Transistor Studios / Quaker Oatmeal

These Quaker Oatmeal ads were developed as part of a larger campaign encouraging users to experiment with various Oatmeal Topping Ideas. I worked as the lead Flash animator and developer on these units at Transistor Studios. I worked closely with the 3D modeling artists, compositors, and art director on the concept and development. The ads were trafficked through EyeWonder.

The Machine Stops

with The Freise Brothers

FreiseBrothers.com was created to showcase the award-winning 3D animation and live-action short film "The Machine Stops" by the Freise Brothers. I developed the site based on designs by Nathan and Adam Freise.

Kayak Feeds

with Google / Kayak

These interactive display ads were developed to visualize RSS feeds from Kayak.com. The units were coded to detect the geographic location of a user, then locate the nearest airport. A list of flights from that airport was then displayed. The units were trafficked through DoubleClick.

Tree

my Personal Project / Cornell University

This motion graphic video was created as a Personal Project to show the effect of life to decay, using a tree as the backdrop. I created four separate drawings, and morphed them together.

Pirates T-Shirt

my Personal Project

This shirt design was a submission for a pirates T-shirt contest. I wanted to capture the playful feeling of children playing pirates, a contrast to the other horror-themed design submissions.

Forest

my Personal Project / School of Visual Arts

This forest scene was created as a prototype for the second scene of a point-and-click fairy tale themed Flash game.
